About the episode

As 2020 finally draws to a close, we're wrapping up this tumultuous year with yet another fantastic guest. This week, we speak to the founder of a Palestine-based sports for development NGO, Tamara Awartani. Tune in as she tells us about the incredible work she has been doing in her homeland Palestine, amid challenging circumstances.

Guests

Tamara Awartani is a consultant and expert in sports for development and sports management sectors, she is the founder of the Palestine Sports for Life NGO, which mission is to empower the Palestinian community through sports on a personal and professional level.
